Staying safe

  • Please stay home if you're sick -- if you're coughing, feverish, or have recently been exposed to someone with COVID-19, do not join the protests and put more people at risk.
  • Wash your hands or sanitize frequently, especially after touching others or shared surfaces.
  • Wear a mask (both to avoid COVID-19 and tear gas).
  • Keep a six-foot distance between yourself and fellow protestors.
  • Do not wear contacts -- both pepper spray and tear gas activate pain-sensing nerves in your eyes.
  • Similarly, avoid makeup or lotion -- they can trap tear gas against your skin and make it more painful.
